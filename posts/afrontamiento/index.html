No Evites el Desafío: El Poder de Afrontar las Situaciones Difíciles

Por Lic. Camila Mastriaco | 02 de Enero, 2025 | Categoría: Resiliencia

Hay momentos en los que sentimos que una situación nos desborda. La reacción instintiva de muchos es evitarla, pensando que así el problema desaparecerá o será menos doloroso. Sin embargo, enfrentar las situaciones difíciles no solo nos ayuda a resolver el problema, sino que también nos permite descubrir nuestra fortaleza interna. Cada paso que das es un avance hacia tu crecimiento.

¿Por Qué Tendemos a Evitar?

La evitación es un mecanismo de defensa natural, pero a largo plazo puede ser perjudicial. Generalmente evitamos por:

  • Fear of failure: The fear of not measuring up or that the outcome will not be as expected paralyzes us.
  • Uncertainty about the future: We prefer to stay in an uncomfortable but known situation rather than face an uncertain future.
  • Feeling of lack of control: Feeling that we do not have the tools or resources to handle the situation leads us to postpone it.
  • Desire to protect ourselves emotionally: We avoid in order not to feel pain, anxiety, or sadness, even if this only delays the inevitable.

4 Claves para Afrontar las Situaciones en Lugar de Huir

  1. Acknowledge your emotions: The first step is to validate what you feel. It is normal to feel fear or anxiety. Allow yourself to feel without judging yourself, but do not let that emotion dictate your actions.
  2. Break the problem into small steps: A big challenge can be overwhelming. Break it down into smaller, more manageable tasks. Focus on completing only the first step. This creates momentum and makes the rest seem less intimidating.
  3. Seek support: You don't have to do it alone. Talking to a friend, family member, or professional can give you a new perspective and the strength you need to move forward.
  4. Focus on what you can control: Often, much of our stress comes from worrying about things that are beyond our control. Identify which aspects of the situation do depend on you and direct your energy towards them.
