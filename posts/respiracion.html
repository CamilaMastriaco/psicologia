Técnica de Respiración 4-7-8: Un Ancla para Calmar tu Mente

Por Lic. Camila Mastriaco | 19 de Octubre, 2024 | Categoría: Autocuidado

En nuestro día a día, es fácil sentir que la mente va a mil por hora. La técnica de respiración 4-7-8 es una herramienta simple y poderosa, popularizada por el Dr. Andrew Weil, que actúa como un tranquilizante natural para el sistema nervioso. No requiere equipo y puede hacerse en cualquier lugar.

Cómo Practicarla: Paso a Paso

  1. Preparation: Sit or lie down in a comfortable position. Place the tip of your tongue behind your upper front teeth, where the roof of your mouth begins. Keep it there throughout the exercise.
  2. Exhale completely: Let all the air out of your lungs through your mouth, making a whooshing sound.
  3. Inhale for 4 counts: Close your mouth and inhale quietly through your nose as you mentally count to 4.
  4. Hold your breath for 7 counts: Hold your breath while you count to 7.
  5. Exhale for 8 counts: Exhale completely through your mouth, making the whooshing sound again, as you count to 8.
  6. Repeat: This completes one breath. Inhale again and repeat the cycle three more times, for a total of four breaths.

¿Qué Beneficios Podés Notar?

Con la práctica regular, esta técnica puede ayudarte a:

  • Reduce anxiety and stress: It helps calm an overactive mind and decrease the fight-or-flight response.
  • Improve sleep quality: It is especially useful for those who have trouble sleeping due to recurring thoughts.
  • Increase mindfulness: It forces you to focus on your breath, bringing you back to the present moment.
  • Improve emotional stability: It gives you a practical tool to manage intense emotions as they arise.
